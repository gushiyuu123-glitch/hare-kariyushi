HARE favicon pack
=================

Generated from the supplied H icon image.
The black outside-corner background has been converted to transparency.

Recommended files to place in /public:
- favicon.ico
- favicon-16x16.png
- favicon-32x32.png
- apple-touch-icon.png
- android-chrome-192x192.png
- android-chrome-512x512.png
- mstile-150x150.png
- site.webmanifest
- browserconfig.xml

Paste the contents of head-favicon-snippet.html inside <head>.

Included extra sizes:
16, 24, 32, 48, 57, 60, 72, 76, 96, 114, 120, 128, 144, 150, 152, 167, 180, 192, 256, 384, 512.
